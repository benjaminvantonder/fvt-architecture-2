Our Slogan

Our inspirational slogan, "A house is a machine for living in," finds its roots in the philosophy of the visionary French architect, Le Corbusier. As one of the most influential figures in modern architecture, Le Corbusier championed the idea that homes should not merely serve as shelters, but as efficient, functional living spaces tailored to the needs of daily life. He was a pioneer in reimagining urban living, introducing the concept of high-density apartment-style housing — a revolutionary shift away from traditional single-family homes. His innovative thinking helped lay the groundwork for much of contemporary architectural practice. Furthermore, Le Corbusier’s bold use of exposed concrete gave rise to the architectural movement known as Brutalism. This unapologetic embrace of raw materials and geometric forms challenged classical aesthetics and sparked widespread debate — a testament to his fearless experimentation and creative vision. Although Brutalism may divide opinions today, the enduring power of his work remains undeniable. Masterpieces like the Villa Savoye, with its clean lines and open plan layout, and the sculptural Chapel of Notre Dame du Haut, with its organic form and spiritual atmosphere, continue to inspire architects and design enthusiasts around the world.

When we employ this slogan, our aim is to underscore the paramount significance of functional architecture. We firmly believe that one's home, where they spend their most cherished moments and invest their precious resources, should be meticulously designed to cater to their specific needs and desires.

About

Francois van Tonder embarked on his architectural journey in 1999, venturing into the realm of small-scale design projects as an independent consultant. Simultaneously, he assumed the esteemed role of a part-time lecturer, imparting his knowledge and expertise in Computer-Aided Design (CAD) to eager minds at the distinguished Technikon Pretoria. His passion for education extended beyond the Department of Architecture, as he dedicated five years to enlightening students in the intricate field of Horticulture at the same revered institution. Furthermore, during the period of 2012-2013, Francois served as a lecturer at the Inscape Education Group, where he adeptly transmitted the intricacies of architectural draughting and architectural technology.

Qualifications

With a B-Tech degree in Architectural Technology, specializing in management, Francois van Tonder stands as a beacon of proficiency and competence in his field. He now assumes the role of a Professional Senior Architectural Technologist, spearheading FVT Architecture as its sole practitioner. Possessing a profound understanding of architectural principles and a keen eye for detail, Francois skillfully navigates the complex intricacies of his craft, crafting spaces that harmoniously blend functionality and aesthetic appeal.

Our Services

Legalization of Existing Building Work ("As-Built" Plans)

Have you made changes to your home or building that haven’t yet been approved by your local municipality? We assist clients in the preparation and submission of "as-built" plans, ensuring compliance with current building regulations and municipal requirements. Our goal is to help you achieve peace of mind knowing your property is legally documented and fully regularized.

New Building Design

Whether you're planning a residential home, a commercial space, or a public structure, we offer full architectural design services tailored to your vision and budget. From concept development and 3D visualization to final construction documentation, we work closely with our clients to bring their ideas to life.

Practical Advice on Design and Materials

Making the right design choices and selecting appropriate building materials is critical to the success and longevity of your project. With years of industry experience, we provide expert advice on practical, cost-effective, and sustainable solutions that align with your aesthetic and functional goals.

Facilitation of Student Practical Studies

We are committed to nurturing the next generation of architects and technologists. Our office offers limited opportunities for architecture students to gain real-world experience through practical training. Interested candidates are encouraged to submit their CVs for consideration. We prioritize passion, eagerness to learn, and a strong foundational understanding of architectural principles.
